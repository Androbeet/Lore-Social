# 📜 LORE — Community Rules & Moderation Policy

*Maintained by admin **aNDROBEET** · contact: andrewz772k6@gmail.com*

## The short version
Be interesting, not harmful. Everything you post is part of the Lore —
make it worth remembering.

## Rules

1. **No harassment or hate.** Attacks on people for who they are
   (race, religion, gender, orientation, nationality) = ban.
2. **No spam.** Repeated identical posts, follow-bombing, vote
   manipulation, or bot behavior gets flagged automatically by the
   Worker's rate limits and reviewed by the admin.
3. **No illegal content.** Obvious.
4. **No impersonation.** One username per human. Pretending to be the
   admin or another user = instant ban.
5. **Tag honestly.** Posts must carry the topic they actually belong to —
   the monthly leaderboards depend on it.
6. **Media links must be safe.** PFPs, voice cards and post images are
   URLs you provide; linking shock content or malware = ban + report to
   the host.

## Enforcement ladder
| Step | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Warning (notification from @androbeet) |
| 2 | Shadowban — your posts visible only to you |
| 3 | Ban — `banned: true` in your user file; the Worker rejects all actions |

## Reporting
Every post and profile has a **… → Report** option. Reports go to a
private queue only the admin sees. False reporting in bulk is itself
rule 2 spam.

## Appeals
Email **andrewz772k6@gmail.com** with your username. The activity log
keeps an append-only history of every action, so appeals are decided on
evidence, not memory.
